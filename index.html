A LITTLE ABOUT ME

As a left-brain/right-brain hybrid (think mathlete/theatre kid), I never thought I'd find a field that challenged me technically and creatively. I currently teach Full-Stack Web Development at App Academy. When I'm not teaching or coding, I can usually be found consuming dangerous quantities of iced coffee and/or adventuring around my beautiful hometown, NYC.
